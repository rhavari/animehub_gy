Corporate Information

AnimeHub was founded in 2023 in New Amsterdam, Berbice by a group of anime enthusiasts who wanted to bring authentic anime culture to Guayana.
What began as a passion project evolved into a thriving hub for collectors and fans.

We specialize in manga, action figures, and limited edition bundles. Our operations serve both local and international markets, with a strong focus on fan experience.

Our Mission

  • Provide high-quality anime and comic merchandise at accessible pricing
  • Support and grow anime fanbase in Guyana
  • Bridge gloabl anime trends with local fan experiences
